Place your custom RPG Tracker preset JSON files in this directory.

Paths:
Your SillyTavern Paths\data\default-user\extensions\rpg-traker-for-sillytavern\presets
or
Your SillyTavern Paths\public\scripts\extensions\third-party\rpg-traker-for-sillytavern\presets

After placing your preset files in the directories above, you can import or load them directly from the Character List UI.
